Programs I use

Priorities

I don't have too harsh priorities, I try my best to not use bloat, but I am also not far enough into it, and wasting time to be a suckless extremist. It'll probably become better in the future, but for now i use rather simple and easy programs.
Here you can find my dotfiles for configs and scripts.

Operating System
I use a minimalist Asahi Linux, which is the Arm Archlinux but on a Apple Silicon M1.
I use it since some weeks, and am very happy with it, can't report any errors, except maybe that i can't change the brightness because the drivers are not working yet.
Terminal
I use Alacritty, i don't have any specific reasons for it, but it just works and is costumizable nicely.
Texteditor
Heavily bloated neovim. It's not bad, certainly the best I've used yet, but I am not happy with the config I have for it, because there is way more than I need.
Window Manager
Bspwm, with Sxhkd as hotkey deamon, I love it, but probably that's just because I have not experienced dmw yet.
File manager
ranger, also need to work on that, it's too heavy python.
