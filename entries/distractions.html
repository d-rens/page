on distractions
As I am sitting here and writing this I am mad with myself.
It is a Thursday summer night and the day nearly over.
The problem I face that I have wasted nearly the entire day, as it so often happens.
And due to what that is, you might ask, it is simple. It is purely the distractions around me. A lot of people don not care about their time, are on social media all day, and just live life as most passively as possible. I was like that too, when I was a chlid very few years ago. But now I changed my way of thinking, so that every minute not used productively is a bad one. I am not on social media anymore, I am most aware of the risks of algoritmically led gratification so to say. But still I am browsing reddit, youtube, discord servers many hours a day, without it seeming to get better, and even less without a reason behind it.
I have addons on firefox to not show me a youtube feed anymore so I can not browse, so I search useless videos instead and watch them then. It has helped, but not as much as I has hoped it would.
Another thing I did was deleting all social media accounts, but I still browse reddit, now just without an account. There seems to be no way to escape.
Obviously there are ways to escape, it is as easy as just not opening a website. But on the other hand there is this primal instinct to get this gratification as minor as it may be, out of pure convenience. It is a horrible thing, it really does not benefit oneself, but so many (obvs. including me) are still doing it.
One reason I write this is the look the problem straight in the eye, to not get around it anymore, constantly making myself think about it, until I actually get it done.
So what are some things I want to do now so it works?
  • As already said, I think there is no way to escape it easily, but first of all being strict with oneself is always an option, if it would just work at least.
  • Using more devices that are less likely to distract. That would basically mean no device with internet access.
