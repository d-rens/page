OS, programs, and everything else

Around three months ago I haven't used any of those mentioned, and there are some good reasons why I sticked to them, even though they required more learning then common programs, but simple enough to get the basics down in a week or so.

The main programs I use are neovim, inkscape and zathura. Neovim being the text-editor, inkscape the vektor drawing program for figures and zathura just the pdf-viewer to look at the outcome.
All those I am using on Asahi linux (arm arch on m1) with bspwm as window manager and sxhkd as hotkey daemon.
This config together with neovim and programs with vim-like keybindings as zathura make working a lot more efficient due to not needing a mouse to be operated.
