Workflow

preface

First of all, my workflow is aimed towards doing maths, and only maths basically. I have a bunch of other subjects, but they either not ought to be noted due to being useless, or are basically the same as math, just with some real-life-applications, as physics.

So one could say it's to write LaTeX.

OS, programs, and everything else

Around three months ago I haven't used any of those mentioned, and there are some good reasons why I sticked to them, even though they required more learning then common programs, but simple enough to get the basics down in a week or so.

The main programs I use are:
neovim
Text editor, a fork of vim, just more modern, and mainly more extensible.
inkscape
Vector drawing program. Using with an inkscape shortcut and figure manager, which i forked of Giles Castel.
zathura
pdf viewer just works, and has vim like keybindings

All those I am using on Archlinux with bspwm as window manager and sxhkd as hotkey daemon.
Due to the programs and operating system it is most efficient, not needing a mouse other than to sometimes draw a figure.
For certain projects I am also required to use the Affinity Suite, or SolidWorks, for those I then use either MacOS or windows. On other computers either for processing power on a windows tower or the display on a macbook for color accuracy.
Both of those should also be able to be virtualized, but the advantages of the other computers are too good to consider that at the moment for me.
